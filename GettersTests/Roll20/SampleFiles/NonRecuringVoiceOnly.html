 OPEN WORLD CAMPAIGN: THE GAME, THE WORLD & THE CAMPAIGN

The game is an Open World Campaign (OWC), a term I created to present an RPG where players join at anytime, anywhere.

The campaign takes place in The Rock, a D&D world with a medieval fantasy setting that I developed to run adventures where the players are the protagonists.

50% Roleplaying & Story / 50% Combat & Exploration.
